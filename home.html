About Me

Salvador Cruz competed at SUNY Brockport, earning All-American honors, serving as captain, and qualifying for nationals twice. He was a 2x conference finalist and 2x all-state wrestler. After competing, Cruz coached at Brockport for 14 years under legendary coach Donald Murray, developing national qualifiers and All-Americans and boosting the program’s success.

A supporter of the sport, he has actively participated in youth and scholastic wrestling programs and camps around Rochester. He continues to mentor and develop the next generation of wrestlers, both on and off the mat. Currently, he has helped multiple varsity-level athletes at Roc Wrestling Club reach their goals, including qualifying for the Class Sectional, State Qualifier, and the New York State tournament.

Instructions to reach McQuaid Jesuit wrestling room.

Location:
McQuaid Jesuit wrestling room at 1800 South Clinton Avenue, Rochester, NY.

Parking Information:
Parking at McQuaid is limited, so carpool if possible. Park at the back of the DiMarco Field House and enter through glass doors labeled "4." If no spots are available, park in front of the school by exit 1 or near exit 12, then walk back to door 4.

Inside the Building:
Enter, go down to the basement, turn left for the cafeteria. The wrestling room is behind the weight room.
