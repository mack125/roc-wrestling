About Me

Salvador Cruz was a competitor at SUNY Brockport. Cruz wrestled at 125 pounds and earned All-American honors during his collegiate career. He served as team captain, a two-time national qualifier, a two-time conference finalist, and a two-time all-state wrestler, building a reputation for discipline, toughness, and technical skill. Following his competitive career, Cruz transitioned into coaching, serving for 14 years as an assistant coach at SUNY Brockport under legendary college coach Donald Murray. In that role, he helped develop national qualifiers and All-American wrestlers, contributing to the program’s success.

A lifelong advocate for the sport, he has also been involved in youth and scholastic wrestling initiatives and camps in the Rochester area, continuing to mentor and shape the next generation of wrestlers both on and off the mat. He has currently assisted in developing multiple varsity-level competitors who attend Roc Wrestling Club, empowering them to achieve their goals of securing placements in the Class Sectional tournament, the State Qualifier tournament, and the New York State tournament.

Please remember the following instructions for getting to McQuaid Jesuit for our season training sessions:

Location:
We practice in the McQuaid Jesuit wrestling room at 1800 South Clinton Avenue, Rochester, NY.

Parking Information:
Parking spots at McQuaid are limited, so we highly encourage carpooling. Upon arrival, please park at the back of the DiMarco Field House and enter through the glass doors labeled "4." If there are no parking spots in the field house lot, you may park in front of the school by exit 1 or on the opposite side of the building near exit 12. From there, please walk back to enter through door 4.

Inside the Building:
Once inside, take the stairs to the basement and turn left to reach the cafeteria. The wrestling room is located behind the weight room.

If you have any further questions or concerns, please feel free to get in touch.
